Summary
Our group plans to look at the relationship between players in the NBA/MLB and their statistics. We will look into players salary, statistic X, age, 
advanceds stats. The goal is to develop a robust database from multiple sources inclusing: sports-reference API, MLB.com, Spotrac, Data.world.

Database: SQL

We will extract the data from the original data sources, transform the datasets with merges and general cleanup and load into a SQL databse

General basketball statistics: https://www.basketball-reference.com
Advanced basketball statistics: https://data.world/fivethirtyeight/nba-raptor-metrics
Contract information: https://www.basketball-reference.com/contracts/players.html
